Places to Visit in Omkareshwar

Omkareshwar Temple

This sacred Jyotirlinga shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva is the heart of Omkareshwar. Devotees from all over the world visit to experience its spiritual energy and stunning location on an island in the Narmada River.

Mamleshwar Temple

An ancient temple known for its unique architecture and divine significance, it is considered as important as the Omkareshwar temple across the river.

Omkaeshwar Dam

The Omkareshwar Dam is a gravity dam on the Narmada River just upstream of Mandhata in Khandwa district, Madhya Pradesh, India.

Kedareshwar Temple

A peaceful temple situated on the banks of the river, perfect for devotees seeking tranquility and meditation by the holy waters.
